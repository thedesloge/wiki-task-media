Flamingo Air is the name of two small seaplane airlines which operate between Florida and the Bahamas.

The first is a small seaplane airline flying in the Bahamas. The airline has 2 bases of operations, one in Grand Bahama International Airport, Freeport and the other at Lynden Pindling International Airport, Nassau. It provides Air Charter services to the Bahamas and south Florida.
